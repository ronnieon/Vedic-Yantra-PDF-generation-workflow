# Example Story Template

Use this as inspiration for your first storybook!

## Story Title
"The Magical Forest Adventure"

## Scenes

### Scene 1
"A young girl named Luna discovers a glowing portal hidden behind an old oak tree in her backyard. She hesitates at the entrance, curiosity battling with fear."

### Scene 2
"Luna steps through the portal and finds herself in a mystical forest filled with bioluminescent plants and floating lanterns. A friendly fox with silver fur approaches her."

### Scene 3
"The silver fox leads Luna to a clearing where a wise old owl sits on a crystal throne. The owl explains that the forest is in danger and needs Luna's help."

### Scene 4
"Luna and the fox embark on a quest to find three magical gems hidden throughout the forest. They encounter a mischievous sprite who tries to trick them."

### Scene 5
"After collecting all three gems, Luna returns to the crystal throne. The owl combines the gems, creating a brilliant light that heals the forest. Luna says goodbye to her new friends."

## Characters
- "Luna: A curious 10-year-old girl with long brown hair, wearing a green adventure vest and carrying a backpack"
- "Silver Fox: A mystical fox with shimmering silver fur and wise blue eyes, guardian of the forest"
- "Forest Owl: An ancient wise owl with white and gold feathers, wearing a crown of leaves"
- "Trickster Sprite: A tiny glowing fairy with purple wings and a mischievous grin"

## Artifacts
- "Glowing Portal: A swirling vortex of blue and purple light between two ancient tree roots"
- "Three Magical Gems: Ruby red, sapphire blue, and emerald green, each pulsing with inner light"
- "Crystal Throne: A majestic throne carved from a single clear crystal, surrounded by floating runes"

## Environments
- "Mystical Forest: Dense woodland with bioluminescent mushrooms, floating lanterns, and trees with glowing leaves"
- "Crystal Clearing: An open space with a crystal throne at the center, surrounded by ancient standing stones"
- "Luna's Backyard: A normal suburban backyard with an old oak tree, fence, and garden"

## Tips for Creating Your Story

1. **Keep scenes concise**: 2-3 sentences per scene works best
2. **Be visual**: Describe what should be seen in the image
3. **Character consistency**: Describe characters in detail so they look the same across scenes
4. **Scene variety**: Mix different locations and actions to keep the story dynamic
5. **Clear narrative arc**: Beginning, middle, and end with a resolution
6. **Express emotions**: Mention how characters feel (curious, brave, scared, wise) - these will be labeled in draft sketches

## Prompt Engineering Tips

### For Better Scene Descriptions
- **Be specific about what's happening**: Describe actions clearly
- **Include visual details**: Colors, lighting, atmosphere
- **Mention character names and emotions**: "Luna feels curious", "The fox appears wise and calm"
- **Describe the environment clearly**: Setting, time of day, weather
- **Background descriptions**: The AI will create text labels for backgrounds in sketches
- **Character emotions are important**: They will be labeled in sketches and rendered in final images
- Keep it concise but descriptive (2-3 sentences works well)

### For Better Character Consistency
- Include specific details: hair color, clothing, accessories
- Use the same description phrases across scenes
- Mention unique identifying features

### For Better Environments
- Specify lighting conditions (e.g., "dappled sunlight", "moonlit")
- Include atmosphere details (e.g., "mysterious", "cheerful")
- Mention key environmental elements

### For Better Overall Images
- Use style keywords: "storybook illustration", "watercolor style", "digital art"
- Specify perspective: "wide shot", "close-up", "over-the-shoulder view"
- Include mood: "whimsical", "dramatic", "peaceful"
